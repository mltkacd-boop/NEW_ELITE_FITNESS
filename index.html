TOJI MODE
Bro Split · Maximum Volume · One Muscle Per Day
✓
🏋️ Intermediate+ 💀 High intensity 🎯 Pure aesthetics
FROM JUJUTSU KAISEN (ANIME/MANGA) DON'T WATCH ANIME? READ THIS ↓
Toji Fushiguro is a character built on one idea: pure physical perfection with zero supernatural ability. While every other character has magic powers, Toji has nothing — just his body. He trained it to such an extreme that he became one of the most dangerous people in the world anyway.
🏆 Real-world equivalent: Think of him as a classic bodybuilder meets elite soldier — someone who built their body through sheer brutal consistency, not shortcuts or genetics.
The training philosophy: One muscle group per day, trained to complete exhaustion. Heavy weight, high volume, no mercy. This is how you build the kind of body that looks powerful even standing still.
💥
Maximum muscle volume per session
6–7 exercises on one muscle = deepest possible stimulus. Most programs don't go this hard on a single muscle.
🔁
Full 7-day recovery per muscle
You destroy chest Monday, it gets 7 days to grow before it's hit again. Maximum repair time = maximum size gains.
🪞
Best for aesthetics & size
Bro split built the physiques you see on stage. Chest day, back day, arms day — this is classic bodybuilding.
🧠
Simple to follow, hard to do
You always know what you're training. Mentally easy to plan. Physically brutal to execute. That's the point.
⚠️
Not for complete beginners. This is high volume training. If you've been training less than 3 months, start with Normal Mode and switch to Toji Mode when your body can handle the intensity.

JIN-WOO MODE
Bro Split · Daily Quests · Hypertrophy Focus
✓
📅 Routine lovers 🎮 Gamification 📈 Hypertrophy
FROM SOLO LEVELING (MANHWA/ANIME) DON'T WATCH ANIME? READ THIS ↓
Sung Jin-Woo starts as the absolute weakest person in a world of monster hunters — so weak people call him "the weakest hunter of all mankind." Then he gets access to a mysterious "System" that gives him daily quests and lets him level up like a video game character. Through relentless daily quests and never skipping a day, he eventually becomes the strongest being on Earth.
🏆 Real-world equivalent: Think of this as the underdog story — starting from zero, levelling up through daily discipline, becoming unrecognisable in a year. The "System" is just structure and accountability.
The training philosophy: Every session is a "quest." Complete it — you level up. Skip it — you fall behind. Same Bro Split as Toji but with higher rep ranges (10–20) focused on muscle growth through volume and pump rather than pure strength.
🎮
Built-in motivation system
Every workout is framed as a quest to complete. XP, levels, ranks — makes showing up feel like an achievement, not a chore.
📈
Higher rep ranges = more muscle
10–20 reps creates more metabolic stress and pump. Research shows this rep range is optimal for muscle size — not just strength.
🗓️
Perfect structure for consistency
You always know what Monday is (chest), what Tuesday is (back). No thinking. Just show up and do the quest.
🔥
Built for the underdog mindset
If you're starting from zero or feel behind — this mode is for you. The whole point is going from weakest to strongest through daily effort.
